Why families choose us

  • Small classes 16 children maximum — less than half the usual, so every child is truly known.
  • An individual plan per child Shared with the family throughout the year.
  • On-site support team Psychologist, special education specialist and speech therapist work inside the school, in partnership with Turbilhão Mágico — therapy happens here, so families don't have to run across the city.
  • English from age 3 Included in the fee; Mandarin available as an extra.
  • Family-friendly hours 8:00–19:00, Monday to Friday, all year round, with holiday camps.
  • The Belém neighbourhood Gardens, museums and the river are our extended classroom.

Learning by doing

Our own method, Aprender Mexendo: children learn through hands-on experience — building, exploring, questioning — and grow into confident, critical thinkers. We teach them how to think, never what to think.

Simple, transparent fees

€390–425/month

Monthly fee (12 months): €390 for kindergarten (ages 3–5) and €425 for primary (ages 6–10), including all curriculum ateliers and hours until 17:30. Meals, extended hours and enrolment fees are itemised — no surprises. Sibling and annual-payment discounts of 5%.
